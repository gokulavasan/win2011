sensitive, links between objects and pointers only hold the field relation.
Now where those relations are made (in which object instantiation) does not
matter, and we add additional noise to the analysis.  For instance:

Our JoeRecord/name in context insensitive analysis points to every name that
was included in a Record instantiation.  This would not be very useful in
determining the alias information found in problem #2.

email addresses.
